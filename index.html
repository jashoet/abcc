About Us

We acknowledge the land we worship on is the Traditional Lands of the Kaurna People. We respect their spiritual and ancestral relationship with their Country. We also acknowledge and recognise the Kaurna People as the right and God's chosen custodians and caretaker of the Adelaide region and that their cultural and heritage beliefs are still as important to the living Kaurna people today.
